Lessons!

Mindful Abstract


Using our emotions to guide us, we can use different materials (whatever you have at home will do!) to create our own Mindful Abstract art piece!

Abstracted Landscape


Gather up any texture paper or make your own textures with different materials to collage an Abstract Landscape!

Shadow Art


Grab some objects from around your house (ones that are see through or that have holes in them work best) and use natural light to create a shadow on your paper. Then we add colors for a beautiful piece!

Positive Affirmation


Let's bring some kindness and positivity to our artwork! Pick a quote or kind/ positive words and with some markers or any materials you want, create a positive affirmation piece.

Still Life Drawing


Take more objects from around your house: groceries, art supplies, toys, etc. and place them on a flat surface in an interesting way! Then, sketch out your objects- focusing on all the details. Once you add color, you'll be just like the famous artists before you- they all studied different objects and painted them!!
